Negate (NEG) Instruction

Purpose

This instruction negates a register, by computing its twos-complement, which is effectively like subtracting its value from zero.

Usage

The following formats are supported for this instruction:

Format Encoding Operation Notes
neg Rd 60H rdsel
temp ← - Rd
Rd ← temp

In this instruction, 'rdsel' selects the destination register, which is the source register.

Flags

The following flags are involved in this instruction:

15 14 13 12 11 10 09 08 07 06 05 04 03 02 01 00
- - NP GP FP IP HE SE GE FE IE V C N P Z
- - - - - - - - - - - * * * * *

Legend: 0 = cleared, 1 = set, ? = tested, * = updated, '-' = n/a
